The SIM system. Anarchy Online's music isn't fixed tracks but Sample-based Interactive Music: each track (called layers, in the SIM tool) is a pool of short clips, and every clip carries a list of the clips allowed to follow it. Specific transition times are defined from each clip to its possible successors, to keep the beat across clips. Together those lists form a directed transition graph. Playback walks the graph, at each step picking the next clip at random from the allowed successors, a track can play indefinitely without ever repeating the same sequence.

For more information, see this article by Bjørn Arve Lagim, one of the composers “The Music of Anarchy Online” on Game Developer.
